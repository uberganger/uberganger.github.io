about

It all started out of the good spirit of collaboration.

Louden Campbell and Nicolas Rhinehart started the project in late 2018. Their ambitions were to start a new form of folk with influences from Justin Vernon, Twain, Shakey Graves, Feist and a couple others. Only a few months after their meeting, the project released its first single “Marlboro Man”. The joy that came from the release started a wildfire in their desire to do more.

After 6 months of work, and the addition of a few more members, their EP "Sly Devil" was released on Sept. 1 2019. It includes rich, soothing, deep folk (The Will), high energy, boot tapping, layered blues (Stirring) and everything in-between. Following the release, reviews were left that can be seen below.

Now, they’ve added on two new members in a desire to go for a new, big, ambitious sound. The band now includes Nicolas Rhinehart (vocals and guitar), Louden Campbell (guitar), Eric Severance (bass), and Matty Garau (percussionist). The album is aimed to release at the end of 2020 and will be their debut piece.
